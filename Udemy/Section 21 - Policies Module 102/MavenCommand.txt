Please use the Maven command for the creation of the custom policy folder.

Command Usage:

"mvn -Parchetype-repository archetype:generate -DarchetypeGroupId=org.mule.tools -DarchetypeArtifactId=api-gateway-custom-policy-archetype -DarchetypeVersion=1.2.0 -Dpackage=mule-policy"

Note: Make sure to remove the double quotes before executing.
